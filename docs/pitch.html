04

The future of business insurance is agentic.

Co-Pilot Era
We are here

AI is used at the margins of insurance. SMEs leverage AI-powered tools to inform decisions; brokers and carriers upgrade customer services chatbots and automate simple process steps in claims. The broking paradigm is unchanged.

Interregnum

Early adopters begin turning over control to agents, allowing access to policy documents, inboxes, and data stores. Agents sometimes interact with insurance providers on SMEs behalf – but mainly through today's web interfaces and without authority to transact.

Agent-to-Agent Era

Agents can take decisions and make payments on SMEs' behalf. All current forms of intermediation (including traditional broking, price comparison, & Google search) are superseded. Carriers and MGAs have MCPs, meaning SMEs' agents can interact with minimal friction based on a new FCA regulatory framework.

10

Agentic Compliance.

Commission-blindness
Denney is designed without consideration of commission rates, eliminating financial bias and ensuring recommendations are in the customer's best interest.
Built-in compliance architecture
Compliance Model serves as a guardrail layer within the software, ensuring all AI outputs are compliant while providing a full audit trail.
Focus on product suitability
Shifting the paradigm to "appropriate cover," Denney proactively identifies & offers cover gaps or overlaps across a customer's entire portfolio to meet customer needs.
Transparency
Denney is an "open book", with a "read only" interface partners can use to evaluate the agentic broker/MGA's integrity and compliance.
Human-in-the-loop
The internal compliance team is dedicated to training and monitoring Denney (rather than processing cases) and staff who interface with it.
