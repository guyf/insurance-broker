01

A £16 Billion Mess*

Business insurance is a mess. Cover is made up of multiple policies from multiple carriers, written in arcane language and shared as pdf attachments. Without the time to unravel it, SMEs are left exposed to under-insurance, duplicate cover, and opportunistic pricing.

* £16 billion is the market size of UK SME insurance in gross written premium

02

The future of business insurance is agentic.

Co-Pilot Era
We are here

AI is used at the margins of insurance. SMEs leverage AI-powered tools to inform decisions; brokers and carriers upgrade customer services chatbots and automate simple process steps in claims. The broking paradigm is unchanged.

Interregnum

Early adopters begin turning over control to agents, allowing access to policy documents, inboxes, and data stores. Agents sometimes interact with insurance providers on SMEs behalf – but mainly through today's web interfaces and without authority to transact.

Agent-to-Agent Era

Agents can take decisions and make payments on SMEs' behalf. All current forms of intermediation (including traditional broking, price comparison, & Google search) are superseded. Carriers and MGAs have MCPs, meaning SMEs' agents can interact with minimal friction based on a new FCA regulatory framework.

Compliance.

AI Broker — Every Customer Conversation

Each session captures: user intent, retrieved policy clauses, recommendations made, products surfaced, and final customer outcome.

↓
Session Recording & Audit Layer

Immutable, timestamped log of every interaction — searchable by session, customer, product type, recommendation, or outcome flag. Full audit trail available to compliance teams and regulators on demand.

↓
Specialist AI Compliance Agents
Mis-selling

Automated scan of every recommendation against stated needs and policy terms. Flags where suggestions exceed guidance or conflict with suitability criteria.

Consumer Duty

Monitors for good outcomes across the four FCA outcomes: products & services, price & value, consumer understanding, and consumer support.

TCF

Post-session checks: loyalty penalties surfaced? Alternatives presented fairly? Customer steered by commission? Scored against TCF outcomes 1–6.

Vulnerable Customers

Detects vulnerability signals (health, life events, financial resilience, capability) per FCA FG21/1. Triggers simplified explanations and escalation.

↓
Ops QA — Humans in the Loop

A configurable % of sessions are flagged for manual review — sampled by AI risk score, product type, or outcome flag. Reviewers work from the audit log, closing the loop between AI output and human sign-off.

Commercial roadmap.

Affiliate Model

Affiliate Model

  • ·Monetize new business referrals to insurance providers through affiliate platforms (e.g. AWIN)
  • ·License insurance product and pricing data from specialist third parties (e.g. Defaqto)
  • ·Build direct insurance relationships needed to establish TOBAs with MGAs and carriers.
Regulatory status: Introducer Appointed Representative
Broker Model

Broker Model

  • ·Monetize new business and renewals primarily through panel of MGAs and carriers.
  • ·Replace data licenses with product and pricing data feeds from panel.
  • ·Develop reputation with carriers by demonstrating profitable portfolio growth near live data feeds
Regulatory status: Appointed Representative then directly authorised by the FCA
Broker-MGA model

Broker-MGA model

  • ·Filter share of new business and renewals to own MGA, insurer panel members, depending on customer's best interest.
  • ·Leverage insights from MGA claims data to optimize filtering.
  • ·Use AI-driven opex savings to enable aggressive new business pricing in priority segments.
Regulatory status: Directly authorised by the FCA
