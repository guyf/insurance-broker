02 / Market Timing

The future of business insurance is agentic

We are at the inflection point — early adopters are beginning to hand control to agents. The broking paradigm is about to be superseded.

Now

Co-Pilot Era

AI is used at the margins. SMEs leverage AI tools to inform decisions; brokers upgrade chatbots and automate simple claims steps. The broking paradigm is unchanged.

Next

Interregnum

Early adopters begin turning over control to agents — access to policy documents, inboxes, and data stores. Agents sometimes interact with providers on SMEs' behalf, mainly through today's web interfaces and without authority to transact.

Future

Agent-to-Agent Era

Agents take decisions and make payments on SMEs' behalf. All current forms of intermediation are superseded. Carriers and MGAs have MCPs — frictionless machine-to-machine interaction under a new FCA regulatory framework.

Denney is being built for the Agent-to-Agent era — launching in the Interregnum, when first-mover advantage is established.

12 / Commercial Model

Commercial roadmap

Three stages from Introducer Appointed Representative to direct FCA authorisation with own MGA.

Stage 1

Affiliate Model

Monetize new business referrals to insurance providers through affiliate platforms (e.g. AWIN). License insurance product and pricing data from specialist third parties (e.g. Defaqto). Build direct insurance relationships needed to establish TOBAs with MGAs and carriers.

Regulatory status: Introducer Appointed Representative

Stage 2

Broker Model

Monetize new business and renewals primarily through panel of MGAs and carriers. Replace data licenses with product and pricing data feeds from panel. Develop reputation with carriers by demonstrating profitable portfolio growth.

Regulatory status: Appointed Representative → directly authorised by the FCA

Stage 3

Broker-MGA Model

Filter share of new business and renewals to own MGA or insurer panel, depending on customer's best interest. Leverage insights from MGA claims data to optimize filtering. Use AI-driven opex savings to enable aggressive new business pricing.

Regulatory status: Directly authorised by the FCA
