07 / Regulatory Framework

Compliance Built In — An AI-First Compliance Model

Every conversation is recorded, structured, and queryable. Compliance is not a manual review process — it is an automated layer that runs continuously against the full interaction log.

🤖 AI Broker — Every Customer Conversation

Each session captures: user intent, retrieved policy clauses, recommendations made, products surfaced, and final customer outcome. Structured metadata tags every interaction at the point of generation.

↓
🗄️ Session Recording & Audit Layer

Immutable, timestamped log of every interaction — searchable by session, customer, product type, recommendation, or outcome flag. Full audit trail available to compliance teams and regulators on demand. No interaction is off the record.

↓
Specialist AI Compliance Agents
🤖 Mis-selling

Automated scan of every recommendation against stated needs and policy terms. Flags where suggestions exceed the guidance boundary or conflict with suitability criteria.

🤖 Consumer Duty

Monitors for good outcomes across the four FCA outcomes: products & services, price & value, consumer understanding, and consumer support.

🤖 TCF

Post-session checks: were loyalty penalties surfaced? Were alternatives presented fairly? Was the customer steered by commission? Scored against TCF outcomes 1–6.

🤖 Vulnerable Customers

Per FCA FG21/1: detects vulnerability signals (health, life events, financial resilience, capability) in session language. Triggers simplified explanations and escalation.

↓
👤 Ops QA — Humans in the Loop

A configurable % of sessions are flagged for manual review by compliance staff — sampled by AI risk score, product type, or outcome flag. Reviewers work directly from the audit log, closing the loop between AI output and human sign-off. Escalations from any AI agent land here first.

08 / How It Works

Architecture — Built to Scale

A production-grade RAG pipeline with agentic tool-use, deployed on serverless infrastructure. Every layer is independently scalable.

🌐 React SPA

Filing cabinet, chat interface, and quote panel. Deployed on Cloudflare Pages with serverless Functions as API proxy.

🖥️ Claude Desktop

Alternative power-user interface via stdio↔HTTP bridge. Same MCP servers, richer tool visibility.

↓
🤖 Claude (claude-sonnet-4-6) — Agentic Tool-Use Loop

Orchestrates multi-step reasoning across both MCP servers. Searches documents, identifies gaps, generates quotes, and explains findings in plain English — all in a single conversation turn.

↓
🔧 Broker MCP Server

Railway-hosted. Tools: semantic search, policy inventory, renewal calendar, market policy ingestion. HTTP endpoints for PDF upload, metadata updates, and document deletion.

💲 Quote MCP Server

Stateless Railway service. Tools: home / motor / pet quotes from 3 insurers, GPT-4o-mini photo analysis for asset identification.

↓
🗄️ Supabase Vector DB

pgvector with HNSW index. JSONB metadata per chunk: policy type, provider, premium, renewal date, insured entity.

⚙️ Ingestion Pipeline

PDF extraction → semantic chunking → OpenAI embeddings (text-embedding-3-small). SHA-256 dedup prevents re-processing.

📄 Policy PDFs

Customer uploads via web UI or local Google Drive sync. Any insurer, any format.

Next Steps & Complications

The prototype is working. Here is what comes next — and what stands between here and market.

Next Steps

2. FCA Permissions

4. Mobile & Messaging Channels

WhatsApp or app interface for renewal alerts and quick coverage questions. Same MCP backend, lower-friction touchpoint.

Complications & Blockers
FCA Authorisation

Insurance distribution requires FCA authorisation or an AR agreement. Until this is in place the product cannot quote, recommend, or place live policies with real customers.

LLM Hallucination Risk

In a compliance-sensitive context, a confidently wrong answer about coverage is a mis-selling event. RAG grounds outputs in actual documents, but edge cases require ongoing red-teaming.

Data Privacy & Storage

Customer policy documents are sensitive personal data. GDPR Article 9 considerations apply. Each user's data must be isolated, encrypted at rest, and subject to deletion on request.

Incumbent Aggregator Contracts

Large retail brands may have exclusive or preferred-panel arrangements with aggregators that limit which insurers can be surfaced. Partner diligence required before launch.
